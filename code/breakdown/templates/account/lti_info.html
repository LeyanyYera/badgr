Add Badges to a Canvas Course

Badgr can connect with Canvas to automatically issue badges to students as they complete their work. In Canvas, set up a course, with modules and completion requirements. Then go to Settings, select Apps, and click the Add App button. Make sure your Badgr account has the email address confirmed that you log in to Canvas with.

Select the Add by URL method, and fill out the form with the information below, filling the Consumer Key, Shared Secret, and Config URL fields.
